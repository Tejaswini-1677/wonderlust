Safari Lodge in the Serengeti

Located in the heart of Serengeti National Park, Tanzania, the Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti offers an immersive experience of African wildlife and nature. Overlooking a popular watering hole, the lodge allows guests to witness elephants, zebras, and other animals up close.

Key Features:

  • Luxury rooms and suites with private balconies
  • Infinity pool with views of the Serengeti
  • Guided game drives with experienced Maasai guides
  • Hot air balloon safaris for breathtaking aerial views
  • Wellness spa and gourmet dining

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit is during the Great Migration (June - October) when millions of wildebeest and zebras cross the Serengeti in search of greener pastures.
