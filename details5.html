Mahagaon, India

Located in the scenic countryside of Maharashtra, India, Mahagaon is a tranquil village surrounded by lush greenery, offering the perfect retreat for nature lovers and those looking for peace away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Key Features:

  • Rustic cottages with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains
  • Traditional Indian cuisine with a farm-to-table experience
  • Experience rural village life and explore nearby farms and temples
  • Guided nature walks and birdwatching tours
  • Peaceful environment for yoga and meditation

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Mahagaon is during the winter season (November - February) when the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for outdoor activities like nature walks, hiking, and exploring the nearby attractions.
